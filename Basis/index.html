About Us

TL Contracting Limited is a private Limited Company, which operates as a professional and reliable team who are "dedicated to working with people" ensuring that each and every client receives a first class service guaranteeing the highest standards of workmanship at competitive rates.

We offer our services to both private and public sectors covering all aspects of construction. The company is on many council approved lists allowing us to carryout works in the Hampshire, Berkshire, and Surrey areas. Our staff are fully skilled but still receive ongoing training as part of our continued growth and ever changing requirements.

Our implementation of a Quality management system has proved the company's commitment to perfection and has led to our organisation achieving the quality standard benchmark of BSI 9001 certification.

The company has 100% safety record and has implemented a set safety procedure ensuring employees, site visitors, and general public are aware of any possible hazards or risks, thus reducing the chance of injury.
